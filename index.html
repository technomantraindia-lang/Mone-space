Understanding the M ONE SPACE difference and how we navigate real estate transactions.

No, we do not operate as traditional, independent brokers. M ONE SPACE is a legally registered real estate advisory and channel partner. We establish direct agreements with leading developers (like Chirag Bhai, Parth Goklani, etc.). For direct developer bookings, you pay zero commission/brokerage, and your invoices are legally structured and GST-compliant.

By limiting our operations to a 5-7 km radius from our office (covering Chandlodiya, Gota, Tragad, Chharodi, Zundal, and surrounding areas), we ensure hyper-local mastery. We monitor construction speed, structural quality, title clearances, and municipality plans in these specific pockets, giving our clients highly precise advice.

We consult on and showcase premium residential structures: modern apartments (2 BHK, 3 BHK, 4 BHK), private duplexes, independent bungalows, and new construction development sites. We also handle rental, corporate leasing, and high-end resale listings in our focus locations.

All payments are made directly to the developer's registered escrow/bank accounts in compliance with RERA guidelines. M ONE SPACE ensures full transparency by guiding you through GST bills, stamp duty, builder-buyer agreements, and the final registrar office documentation.
