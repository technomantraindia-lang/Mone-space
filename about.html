About Us

Elevating Real Estate with Precision Advisory

Founded in June 2026 by **Maulik Sureshbhai Patel**, M ONE SPACE emerged to solve a critical issue in Ahmedabad's property market: standard brokerage friction. We envisioned a client-first agency structured entirely as an authorized corporate channel partner.

By combining expert documentation checks, hyper-local monitoring, and builder direct prices, we help families and companies secure premium assets with absolute peace of mind.

We believe buying real estate should be an inspiring milestone, not a stressful transaction. Our core philosophy is centered on **radical transparency** and **fiduciary responsibility**.

We refuse traditional commissions from direct buyers. Operating with clean agreements, certified developer partnerships, and standard escrow routes, we establish a professional standard of trust.

Our operational framework is deliberately restricted to a **5 to 7 km radius** from our central office. This hyper-local focus ensures we monitor builder speeds, title clearances, and municipal zoning updates in real-time.

We integrate directly with developers' backend inventory management systems, giving our clients first-access pricing, premium RERA validations, and zero resale overheads.
